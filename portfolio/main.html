What is a portfolio resume?

A portfolio resume is a document that contains samples of your previous work to help showcase your talents and capabilities. Rather than simply explaining your achievements, this resume type shows past projects, helping you to better impress the hiring manager. It also allows you to showcase details that an ordinary resume would be unable to display.

Resume portfolios often merge a resume and a portfolio on a single page, but this requires creativity and design knowledge. More traditional professionals or creatives applying to traditional workplaces can choose to have separate pages for their resumes and portfolio.

While people often associate portfolios with creatives, other professionals can benefit from their use. Whether you're a web developer showing samples of your work or a teacher who wants to demonstrate the effectiveness of their class notes and assessments, you can use a resume portfolio to give yourself a competitive advantage.
